Between Us

Released November 2021

Musicians:

  • Perry Edwards, Vocals
  • Leigh-Anne Pinnock, Vocals
  • Jade Thirlwall, Vocals

Between Us is the first greatest hits album by British girl group Little Mix, released on 12 November 2021 through RCA. The album has been described by the group as a celebration of sisterhood, friendship and fun.[1] It was supported by two singles, "Love (Sweet Love)" and "No". The standard edition of the album which consists of twenty-two tracks, includes eighteen of the group's previously released singles, including four of their five number-one singles, "Wings", "Black Magic", "Shout Out to My Ex", and "Sweet Melody".

Confetti

Released November 2020

Musicians:

  • Perry Edwards, Vocals
  • Leigh-Anne Pinnock, Vocals
  • Jade Thirlwall, Vocals

Musically, Confetti, is primarily a pop and R&B record with elements of 1980s pop, 2000s pop, and gospel music. Upon release the album was met with positive reviews from critics with some describing it as a solid pop album with praise given to its production.[1] The album addresses themes of self-acceptance, self-worth, freedom, and independence. The album was supported by four singles; "Break Up Song", "Holiday", "Sweet Melody", became the group's fifth number-one single in the United Kingdom and a remix of the title track, featuring Saweetie.
